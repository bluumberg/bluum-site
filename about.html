Our Purpose

Turning Recovery Into an Adventure

Bluum transforms pediatric rehabilitation into an engaging, interactive experience. Through gamification, we help children stay motivated during their inpatient recovery by turning exercises and tasks into fun rewarding challenges.

What is Bluum?

A digital ecosystem designed to enhance pediatric rehabilitation

Bluum transforms rehabilitation into an engaging adventure where children create personalized avatars, earn rewards by completing recovery tasks, customize virtual spaces, and connect with peers through interactive leaderboards.

Simultaneously, healthcare providers are able to monitor progress and manage personalized rehab plans through a companion web portal.

Why Bluum?

We Make Rehabilitation Engaging, Motivating, and Effective

Pediatric rehabilitation presents unique challenges for both young patients and healthcare providers. Children often find traditional rehabilitation repetitive and demotivating, leading to inconsistent program adherence. Meanwhile, healthcare professionals struggle to effectively track patient progress and maintain engagement.

Bluum bridges the gap between medical necessity and childhood engagement, making rehabilitation not just bearable, but enjoyable, leading to better outcomes and a more positive experience for everyone.

How Bluum Works?

We transform the rehabilitation experience through:

Engaging Gamification

Transforming essential rehabilitation tasks into exciting adventures with virtual rewards and achievements, creating a motivational system that fosters positive reinforcement and gives children a tangible sense of progress.

Fostering Community

Creating a supportive environment where young patients connect with fellow peers in the patient unit through an interactive leaderboard that allows them to encourage and follow each other's progress, all while adhering to HIPAA compliances.

Medical Oversight

Equipping healthcare professionals with intuitive easy to use interfaces to manage patient activities, assign personalized tasks, and monitor real-time progress quickly and effortlessly.

Core Values

Our core values at Bluum guide every decision we make and shape our company culture.

Empowerment.

We give children agency in their recovery process.

Children own their healing journey through personalized avatars, rewards, and progress tracking. Our gamified approach makes recovery engaging, builds resilience, and improves outcomes while boosting moral.

Collaboration.

Partnering with experts to deliver clinically sound solutions.

Medical expertise is essential in developing tools that truly benefit patients. We maintain partnerships with hospitals and consult regularly with our advisory board of pediatric specialists to align technology with best practices in care.

Compassion.

Caring deeply for each child's unique recovery needs.

Compassion connects us to the real children behind each recovery. We understand pediatric experiences through direct engagement, storytelling, and programs that bring our team closer to the children we serve.
